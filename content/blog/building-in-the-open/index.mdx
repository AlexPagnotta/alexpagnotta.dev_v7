Working in public changes the incentives. When the process is visible, you optimize
for *shipping* rather than for looking finished.

## Why it works

- Feedback arrives while it can still change the outcome.
- The archive of small posts becomes the portfolio.
- Momentum compounds — each shipped thing lowers the bar for the next.

Start before you feel ready. The readiness comes from the shipping.
